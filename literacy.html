Analyzing the Genre: Model Analysis 2

Selection

This assigment focused on reading a research paper that looked at the correlation between lawyers and depression. This study was done to answer a question of what causes lawyers to become burntout and feel a lot of stress. This was another good example of a structure towards conducting research, and I was able to make parallels between Model Analysis 1 and this article which has helped to find guidelines for writing a research paper.

Reflection

After writing this assigment I looked back at Model Analysis 1 to make parallels and to figure out what are some of the key structural elements that are present in both research papers. I found it beneficial to do this as the two papers focused on different topics, but some elements were similar, like the showcase of a comment made by a person who is engaging in the topic and the analysis that followed the comment. I used this structure in Writing Project 2.
